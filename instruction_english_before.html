Instructions for the Candidates
Sr No Typing Language Time Duration (in Minutes)  
1 English 15 There will be 10 minutes time interval between both tests
  1. Before the start of typing test, candidate will be given 2 mins to check the functioning of all the keys on the keyboard. In-case of any problem, immediately inform the invigilator
  2. Any issues raised will not be entertained after clicking the Check Box to confirm that they have tested the keyboard and it is working fine
  3. The passage will be 500 words. The speed required for English typing is 30 w.p.m.
  4. Following Keys will be disabled during the typing test:
    • Escape Key
    • All function Keys (F1 to F12)
    • TAB
    • CAPS Lock
    • Windows Key (Left & Right)
    • CTRL Key (Left +Right)
    • Alt + F4
    • Insert
    • Delete
    • Home
    • End
    • Page Up
    • Page Down
    • Copy and paste option will be disabled
  5. Use of the mouse is not permissible during typing, except to go to the next page
  6. To start the typing test, click on "I am ready to Begin" button
  7. During the examination, the remaining time will be displayed on the top right corner of the computer screen
  8. Typing Test will be automatically submitted after the stipulated time
  9. Once the Typing Test is successfully submitted, candidates will be able to see the typed content on the preview screen.
  10. The candidate will have to be seated in their place and will not be allowed to leave the examination hall until the entire process of examination is completed.
  11. Exam will be cancelled for those candidates who are caught seeking help from other candidates, disturbing the atmosphere of the examination, or attempting to take the examination for someone else.
  12. Taking recourse to unfair means or attempting to influence in any manner any person authorized to conduct the examination for the results of the examination is a serious offense and appropriate legal action will be taken against the candidate.
I have read and understood all the above instructions. I agree that I have not brought with me any prohibited item like mobile phone etc. to the examination hall. I agree that in case of non-compliance of the instructions, I will be disqualified from taking the typing examination.
